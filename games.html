GAMES

Flower Power

A 2D platformer level created with Unity. The player must navigate past spiky enemies and obstacles, with the help of collectible items, to reach the level's end goal.

Art Room Adventure

A 3D adventure-style game created with Unity. The player has been shrunk down and must explore an art studio to find the missing pieces of a painting and solve the puzzle to escape.

All of the game assets were scanned in using Polycam and touched up in Blender.
